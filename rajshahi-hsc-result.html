About Rajshahi Board

Rajshahi Board manages the Rajshahi Division and consistently records strong pass rates and GPA-5 achievements.

How To Check Online

  1. Go to the official portal: educationboardresults.gov.bd
  2. Select exam: Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC), Year: 2026
  3. Choose Board: Rajshahi
  4. Enter your Roll Number and Registration Number, complete the security check, and submit
Go To Official Result Portal →

How To Check By SMS

If the website is slow on result day, use SMS as a reliable backup:

HSC RAJSHAHI <Roll Number> 2026

Send to 16222 from any operator (Teletalk, GP, Robi, Banglalink, Airtel). You'll receive your GPA by reply SMS.
