Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BDExam ("we", "our", "the site") collects, uses, and protects information when you use bdexam.pages.dev.

Information We Collect

Our calculators run entirely in your browser — subject marks and grades you enter are never sent to our servers or stored by us. We do not collect roll numbers, registration numbers, or any personally identifying academic information.

  • Standard technical data (browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited) collected automatically via analytics
  • Cookies and similar technologies used by our advertising partners

Advertising & Cookies

BDExam is supported by third-party advertising networks, which may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. These partners may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Links

This site links to the official Bangladesh Education Board result portal and other external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Children's Privacy

While many BDExam visitors are students, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and no account or personal data is required to use any calculator.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so may affect how ads are displayed but will not affect calculator functionality.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent via our Contact page.
