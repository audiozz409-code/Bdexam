About BDExam

BDExam was built to solve two problems at once: giving students an accurate, transparent GPA calculator that correctly applies the Bangladesh Education Board's 4th-subject bonus rule, and giving them a clear, honest guide to actually checking their real, official result — without pretending to be a live results database ourselves.

Our GPA calculators use the official Bangladesh Education Board grading scale (A+ at 80-100 marks down to F below 33) and the standard compulsory-subject-plus-bonus formula used nationally across SSC, HSC, Dakhil, Alim, and equivalent examinations.

BDExam is independently run and not affiliated with any Education Board, the Ministry of Education, or educationboardresults.gov.bd. For your actual official result, always use the government's own portal or SMS service, linked clearly throughout this site. BDExam is a free public utility, supported by advertising.
