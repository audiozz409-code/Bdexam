Free exam and education calculator tools built for Bangladeshi students — SSC, HSC, university and beyond.

What is BDExam?

BDExam is a free online platform providing education and career calculators specifically designed for students and job seekers in Bangladesh. From calculating your SSC or HSC GPA under the Bangladesh Education Board grading system to checking government job salary scales, BDExam gives you accurate results in seconds.

All tools are completely free. No login required. Works on any device — mobile, tablet, or desktop.

Built for Bangladesh Students

Our GPA calculators follow the official grading system established by the Bangladesh Ministry of Education — the same system used by all education boards including Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi, Sylhet, Comilla, Jessore, Dinajpur, Mymensingh, Barishal, and the Madrasah Board.

The government job salary tool is based on the National Pay Scale 2015, the official pay structure still in use for Bangladesh civil service employees in 2026.

Accuracy & Updates

Our calculators are based on official sources including:

  • Bangladesh Education Board grading system (Ministry of Education)
  • National Pay Scale 2015 (Ministry of Finance)
  • University Grants Commission of Bangladesh (UGC) CGPA guidelines
  • Government scholarship schemes (Board Talent Pool, PM Fellowship)

We update our tools whenever official changes are announced.
